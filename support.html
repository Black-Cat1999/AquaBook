Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own cue? +

No, we provide high-quality, balanced house cues and fresh chalk for all visitors at the reception.

Can I cancel my table booking? +

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before your slot for a full refund. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable.

Are the tables perfectly leveled? +

Absolutely. Our 8-ball and snooker tables are professionally leveled weekly to ensure tournament-grade play.
